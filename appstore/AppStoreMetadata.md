## Description

LastTick is a philosophical tool for time awareness and existential reflection.

Ever wonder how much time you truly have? LastTick provides an estimated countdown of your remaining life—not to create fear, but to inspire presence, intention, and gratitude for each moment.

**Features:**
• Personalized life expectancy estimate based on health factors
• Real-time countdown: Years, Months, Days, Hours, Minutes, Seconds
• Beautiful circular progress visualization
• Daily philosophical quotes (Stoic, realistic, gentle tones)
• Optional numerology & astrology adjustments
• Three stunning themes: Light, Dark, Void
• Local data storage - complete privacy
• Daily reminder notifications
• No ads, no tracking, no data collection

**Philosophy:**
Inspired by Stoic wisdom and the ancient practice of "Memento Mori" (remember you must die), LastTick helps you:
- Live with greater intention
- Appreciate the present moment
- Prioritize what truly matters
- Reduce time wasted on trivial concerns

**Important Disclaimer:**
This app is for philosophical reflection only. Life expectancy estimates are illustrative and based on statistical averages. This is NOT medical advice. Consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

**Privacy:**
All your data is stored locally on your device only. We never collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

**Perfect for:**
- Philosophy enthusiasts
- Mindfulness practitioners
- Stoicism students
- Anyone seeking more intentional living
- Those interested in existential reflection

## App Review Notes

**To the App Review Team:**

Thank you for reviewing LastTick.

**What this app does:**
LastTick is a philosophical reflection tool that provides users with an illustrative estimate of their remaining life expectancy. The purpose is to encourage mindfulness and intentional living, inspired by Stoic philosophy and the practice of "Memento Mori."

**How it works:**
1. Users input basic health information (smoking, exercise, sleep, diet, stress levels)
2. Optionally, users can include numerological/astrological data (birth time, place)
3. The app calculates an estimated life expectancy using statistical adjustments
4. A real-time countdown displays estimated time remaining
5. Daily philosophical quotes inspire reflection

**Important disclaimers:**
- The app clearly states it is NOT medical advice (shown during onboarding and in About section)
- All estimates are illustrative only and based on statistical averages
- Data is stored locally on device only—no server transmission
- The app's purpose is philosophical reflection, not medical diagnosis

**Privacy:**
